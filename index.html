Mustache Me

A grid-based puzzle game where you are a mustache and use a combination of mathematical skills and guessing to find a hidden potato.
You use baby potatoes who follow you creating a wall as well and angry, non-moving potatoes who don't let anyone through them
to trap the potato.
Over 100 levels with 5 different game modes.
